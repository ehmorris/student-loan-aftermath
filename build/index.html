Northeastern Aid

Some students get more grant money and scholorships than others, and some have parents that can pay whatever's left, but assuming neither of those is true, you're left with $85645 to make-up. That's where loans come in. If you get your FAFSA right and you can get all your loans from the government, you'll usually have a 50/50 split between subsidized and unsubsidized loans.
